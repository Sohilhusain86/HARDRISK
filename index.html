Internet ki Chhupi se Chhupi Jankari

Yeh page internet ke kam jane jate tathya aur rochak jankari ko cover karta hai, jo aam taur par charcha mein nahi aate.

1. Internet ka Structure

  • Surface Web: Yeh woh internet hai jo Google, Bing ya Yahoo ke through access hota hai. Ye internet ka sirf 4-5% hai.
  • Deep Web: Internet ka woh hissa jo search engines se indexed nahi hota, jaise private databases, academic journals, aur government records. Ye ~90% hai.
  • Dark Web: Deepè‹—Deep web ka ek chhota, anonymized hissa, jo Tor network ya I2P ke through access hota hai. Yahan illegal activities ke sath-sath whistleblower platforms bhi hain.

2. Chhupi Technologies aur Protocols

  • TOR (The Onion Router): Anonymous browsing ke liye istemal hota hai. Data ko multiple layers mein encrypt karta hai.
  • Blockchain-based Networks: Decentralized networks jo cryptocurrencies aur Web3 applications ko power dete hain.
  • ZeroNet/I2P: Peer-to-peer networks jo censorship-resistant hote hain aur traditional servers ke bagair kaam karte hain.

3. Data Tracking aur Privacy

  • Cookies aur Trackers: Har website aapke browsing behavior ko track karti hai. Third-party cookies ads ke liye data collect karte hain.
  • Fingerprinting: Websites aapke browser, device, aur settings ke unique patterns se aapki pehchan karti hain.
  • Data Brokers: Companies jaise Acxiom aur Experian aapka personal data collect aur sell karti hain.

4. Cybersecurity ke Chhupe Tathya

  • Zero-Day Exploits: Hackers software ke unknown vulnerabilities ka fayda uthate hain.
  • Botnets: Lakhs of infected devices ek network mein judkar DDoS attacks ya spam campaigns chalate hain.
  • Phishing ke Naye Roop: AI-generated emails aur deepfake videos se phishing attacks ab aur sophisticated ho gaye hain.

5. Internet ke Social aur Cultural Aspects

  • Echo Chambers: Algorithms aapko wahi content dikhate hain jo aapke views se match karta hai, jisse polarization badhta hai.
  • Meme Culture aur Virality: Internet ke subcultures (jaise Reddit, 4chan) global trends aur memes ko shape karte hain.
  • Disinformation Campaigns: Governments aur organizations social media par propaganda aur fake news spread karte hain.

6. Dark Web ki Haqeeqat

  • Dark web sirf illegal activities ke liye nahi hai. Yahan journalists, activists, aur privacy advocates bhi active hain.
  • Markets jaise Silk Road ke successors abhi bhi exist karte hain, lekin law enforcement inhe regularly target karta hai.
  • Cryptocurrency (jaise Bitcoin, Monero) dark web transactions ka backbone hai.

7. Internet ka Future

  • Web3: Decentralized internet jo blockchain par based hai, jahan users apna data control kar sakte hain.
  • Quantum Internet: Quantum computing ke sath internet ki speed aur security badh sakti hai.
  • Metaverse: Virtual reality aur augmented reality ke sath internet ek immersive experience ban sakta hai.

8. Kuch Interesting Facts

  • Internet ka pehla "website" 1991 mein CERN ne banaya tha.
  • Har second ~40,000 Google searches hote hain.
  • Internet ka ~30% traffic adult content se related hai.
  • Undersea cables internet ka backbone hain, jo 99% international data carry karte hain.
